Lasagna

Description

This is an Italian recipe that is common around the world. It contains pasta, cheese, meat and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • pasta
  • cheese
  • meat

How to Make Lasagna Step by Step

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Layer in meat, cheese, and sauce
  3. Bake for one hour at 450 degrees
  4. Enjoy!
